Group Classes

We offer a range of classes such as HIIT, aerobic, conditioning, body/mind and dance. Each class is suitable for all levels from high to low intensity so every participant is free to choose the challenge. We also offer choice of options and guidance with belief it will encourage you to test your bravery. The aim is to bring the enjoyment and unity within community regardless of shape and fitness level.

  • HIIT – High intensity interval training toning and strengthening the body, intermediate/advanced level
  • Cardio/Aerobic – Combination of old school exercises and aerobic moves improving cardio-vascular and respiratory condition.
  • Conditioning/Strength and Tone – Improving muscle and joints strength and endurance by performing upper and lower body training
  • Zumba/Clubbercise – Great dance fun from Cumbia, Reaggeton and Salsa rhythms to clubb classics.
  • Balance/Stretch – Combination of balance and stretch exercises based on Yoga, Pilates and Thai-Chi improving flexibility and range of movement.

One to One Sessions

If you would like to have full attention we offer 1:1 sessions. They will be adapted to your individual goal and level. Wherever you are on your fitness journey, we will provide a thorough guideline and support to help you achieve the best possible results. To help you commit better we will do our best to keep the surprises and excitement within our programme.

Kinetic Chain Release

Kinetic chain release, designed/developed by Hugh Gilbert, is medically approved series of gentle joint mobilisations and muscle stretches bringing body back to balance and health. It also helps to release chronic pain and discomfort as well as chronic stress. It may often eliminates other conditions such insomnia and IBS.
